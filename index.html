About

My name is Geske. I am a rapper and engineer based in New Orleans. My sound is a mixture of artists such as Cordae, Mac Miller, and Jack Harlow. If you want to hear the whole project, you can sign up for my email list using the same link and going to the home page. By typing in your emial, you will receive a free download.
